Robotics

One of the best ways to express creativity and imagination is through innovative digital platforms. The girls will explore the latest edutech tools that will foster the love for tinkering and robotics in them. Modules will be run over period of few weeks to help girls learn about the endless possibilities of robotics by working with Sphero SPRK+, Bloxels, Microbit, Lego Boost and Makeblock Ultimate 2.0 to name a few.​

Coding

Coding nurtures logical thinking and creativity. It doesn't have to be stressful or boring. Our creative coders will have lots of fun and discover the joy of coding in a collaborative and context-based setting where they will learn the basics of computational thinking and programming. Exposure to coding in this manner will increase the participation of girls in computer science and build their interest over a period of time.

Engineering Challenge

This challenge is an authentic STEM experience in which girls will engage in inquiry-based projects with well-defined learning objectives in math and science using the engineering design process. ​These projects typically take 2-4 lessons to complete. The girls will step into the shoes of engineers, display design thinking, build prototypes and tackle problems that are pertinent in the real world.

Why join The Lavender Spaceship Project?

Introduce your primary and middle school girls to the exciting world of STEM where they solve problems in context, build prototypes, code and tinker with the latest technological tools and software. Our goal is to show the girls that STEM is exciting, doable and full of creativity. It doesn't have to be overwhelming or boring. They will work collaboratively, share ideas and have loads of fun. ​ At The Lavender Spaceship Project, we provide an environment where girls experience a sense of social belongingness. They are encouraged, motivated and become aware of more girls like them from around the world who are doing amazing work in STEM. Our learning platform at The Lavender Spaceship project not only develops the passion for science and technology, it also builds a community of young girls who will help spread the enthusiasm for STEM, alter perceptions of career choices for women and foster progressive mindsets.
